SUBMIT YOUR WORK THE PRESS About Snowy Plover Press Snowy Plover Press is an independent literary press focused on distinctive projects, poetry chapbooks, and print work published for nonconforming minds. We publish work that emerges from a neurodivergent perspective: poetry, correspondence, field notes, non-fiction, and fiction. The press takes its name from the snowy plover, a small shorebird that camouflages itself in plain site, always at the margin of the crowd, but close enough to observe us. A serious researcher, the snowy plover has simply never bothered to publish, until now.
