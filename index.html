Snowy Plover Dispatches

C.J. Ramos Letters is a six-issue mailed publication from Snowy Plover Press. Each month, subscribers receive a new printed letter from C.J. Ramos, an anthropological researcher studying the habits, contradictions, and social rituals of Neurotypicals.

Smart, strange, and quietly funny, the letters are meant to be read one at a time and followed over the course of the series — as a larger pattern slowly begins to emerge.

The letters are for people who enjoy a different point of view and the pleasure of receiving real mail worth keeping.

Each monthly mailing includes

  • One printed letter from C.J. Ramos
  • One paper insert — a mystery note fragment
  • A collectible field card or word search activity
  • A bookmark with field observations

About Snowy Plover Press

Snowy Plover Press is an independent literary press focused on distinctive projects, poetry chapbooks, and carefully made print work — published for nonconforming minds.

We publish work that emerges from a neurodivergent perspective: poetry, correspondence, field notes, and fiction that sees the world sideways and finds something worth saying about it.

The press takes its name from the snowy plover — a small shorebird that stations itself at the margin of the crowd, close enough to observe, far enough to remain unnoticed. A serious researcher. It has simply never bothered to publish.
